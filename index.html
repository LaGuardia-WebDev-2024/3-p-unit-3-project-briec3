  • Some Other Dubai Chocolate Recipes!

    • First Recipe: Labubu Flavored Dubai Chocolate Bar!
    Ingredients Quanity
    Labubu Fur: 5 Labubus Worth
    Pistachio Butter: 8 Ounces
    Milk Chocolate Chips: 12 Ounces

      Step 1: Gather Your Labubu Fur!

      Step 2: Put your Melted Chocolate Chips in an Ice Tray!

      Step 3: Put Your Pistacio Butter On Top!

      Step 4: Put Your Labubu Fur On top of the Pistacio Butter In Tray!

      Step 5: Put Your Melted Chocolate On The Tray!

      Step 6: Freeze Overnight!

      Second Recipe: Matcha Dubai Chocolate!
      Ingredients Quanity
      Matcha Powder: 3 Cups
      Milk: 3 Cups
      Pistachio Butter: 8 Ounces
      Milk Chocolate Chips: 12 Ounces

      Step 1: Pour Milk Into Bowl!

      Step 2: Pour Matcha Powder Into Bowl!

      Step 3: Melt Your Chocolate Chips!

      Step 4: Put Chocolate Chips Into Ice Tray!!

      Step 5: Put Your Matcha On Top Tray!

      Step 6: Put Your Melted Chocolate As A Coat!

      Step 7: Freeze Over Night!
